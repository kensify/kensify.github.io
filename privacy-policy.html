Privacy Policy for Kensify Apps

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Kensify collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our educational Android applications, including KPSC Exam Preparation App, KSP PC PSI Exam App, Most Amazing Facts, Verbal Memory Test, and other Kensify apps.

Information We Collect

Kensify apps may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Kensify apps do not require personal identification to use, but if you voluntarily provide information through feedback forms or contact us sections, it may include your name and email address.
  • Non-Personal Information: This includes device type, app version, operating system, and usage statistics to help us improve the app experience.
  • Cookies and Analytics: We may use Google Analytics for Firebase to track app performance and usage data. No personal identification is linked to this data.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for:

  • Improving app functionality and performance.
  • Providing a better learning experience and personalized content.
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance app features.
  • Communicating important updates, announcements, and promotional messages if you opt-in.

Third-Party Services

Kensify apps may use third-party services for analytics, advertising, and app performance, such as:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • AdMob (for ads in free apps)

These third-party services may collect information sent by your device, including device identifiers, app usage data, and IP addresses. Kensify does not control these services and recommends reviewing their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

We prioritize your data security. While no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, Kensify employs industry-standard practices to safeguard your information and protect it from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Children’s Privacy

Kensify apps are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided such information, please contact us immediately at contact@kensify.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Kensify may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app and/or on our website. Continued use of our apps after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
