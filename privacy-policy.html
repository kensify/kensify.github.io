Privacy Policy — Kensify

Effective for Kensify apps (e.g. KPSC Prep, Amazing Facts, Verbal Memory Test and related educational apps).

Summary

Kensify ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our mobile applications, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Email address or name only if you contact us or sign up for newsletters.
  • Device & Usage Data: Device model, OS version, app usage statistics, crashes, and performance data to improve the app.
  • Content You Provide: Any text or files you enter or upload (e.g., feedback, profile info).
  • Third-party Data: Data from ad networks and analytics platforms (see "Third-party Services").

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain and improve the apps and services.
  • To respond to support requests and contact you about updates, changes or promotions (only if you opt in).
  • To analyze usage patterns and monitor app performance and crashes.
  • To personalize ads shown to you through third-party ad networks (when enabled).

3. Ads & Analytics

We may use advertising networks and analytics services (for example Google Play services, Firebase Analytics, AdMob, or similar). These services collect information about your interactions to help deliver relevant ads and improve the product.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share anonymized, aggregated usage data with service providers and partners.
  • We may disclose data to comply with legal obligations or to protect our rights or users' safety.

5. Data Retention & Security

We keep your data only as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, but no system is completely secure — if a breach occurs we will follow applicable laws and notify affected users when required.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are intended for general educational use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Choices

  • Opt out of targeted advertising through your device settings or within the ad provider's settings.
  • Turn off analytics collection where permitted by your device or the app.
  • Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information we hold about you.

8. Third-party Links & Services

Our apps may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. Please review their privacy policies before providing personal information.
