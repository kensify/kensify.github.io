Privacy Policy

Protecting your data is our top priority at Kensify.

Introduction

Kensify built its educational apps as free services. This page explains how we handle information and protect user privacy. By using our apps, you agree to this policy.

Information Collection and Use

Our apps are designed for education and learning. Kensify does not collect personal data directly. However, integrated third-party services may collect limited data for analytics or ads.

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (for responsible ads)
  • Firebase Analytics

Each third-party follows its own privacy policy, accessible on their official websites.

Log Data

If an error occurs, we collect diagnostic data through third-party tools. This may include your device model, IP address, OS version, and usage time — purely for performance improvement.

Cookies

Our apps do not directly use cookies. However, third-party SDKs might use them for analytics or ad optimization. You can disable cookies through your device settings if you prefer.

Security

We use industry-standard security practices to keep your data safe. However, no digital method is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are meant for learners of all ages but do not target children under 13 for personal data collection. If you believe your child has shared data with us, contact us immediately at support-kensify@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any updates will be reflected here with a revised effective date.
